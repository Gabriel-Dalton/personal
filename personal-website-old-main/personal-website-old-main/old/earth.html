My Story

"A childhood spent navigating open waters."

I was born on a boat and spent many years living on it, learning to appreciate the world from a unique perspective. Living on the water taught me resilience, adaptability, and the importance of sustainable living.

At a young age, I became fascinated with technology and self-taught myself multiple coding languages, building my first website while still a child. My passion for innovation grew alongside my curiosity about environmental impact, driving me to merge both fields.

My father, an entrepreneur himself, introduced me to the world of business, teaching me invaluable lessons about leadership, sustainability, and creating impactful change. With these foundations, I launched Oasis of Change Inc., a nonprofit dedicated to building a greener digital landscape by reducing website carbon footprints and supporting environmental initiatives worldwide.
